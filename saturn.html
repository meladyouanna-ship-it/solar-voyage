Saturn

  • Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in our solar system.
  • It is known for its prominent ring system, which is composed of ice particles, rock debris, and dust.
  • Like Jupiter, Saturn is a gas giant with a thick atmosphere of hydrogen and helium.
  • It has a complex system of moons, with Titan being the largest and most Earth-like in terms of size and atmospheric conditions.
