Uranus

  • Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and the third-largest in our solar system.
  • It is known for its unique sideways rotation and a cold, windy atmosphere.
  • Like other gas giants, Uranus is primarily composed of hydrogen and helium.
  • It has a system of rings and moons, with Miranda, Ariel, Umbriel, Titania, and Oberon being the largest.
