Jupiter

  • Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system, famous for its colorful bands of hydrogen and helium clouds.
  • It features a massive, centuries-old counterclockwise storm that is wider than the entire diameter of Earth.
  • It acts like its own system, possessing a faint ring structure and a vast family of over ninety moons.
  • It spins incredibly fast, generating an immense magnetic field that traps intense radiation belts around it.
